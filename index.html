Study Tips & Tricks

  • Break your study sessions into focused intervals (Pomodoro technique).
  • Remove distractions by turning off notifications.
  • Use active recall and spaced repetition for better retention.
  • Stay hydrated and take short breaks regularly.
  • Set clear, achievable goals for each study session.
  • Mix up subjects to keep your brain engaged.
  • Review your notes regularly rather than cramming.
